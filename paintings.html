The Starry Night

Painted by Vincent van Gogh in 1889, The Starry Night is famous for its swirling sky, bright stars, and expressive style. It is one of the most recognized paintings in the world.

Mona Lisa

Painted by Leonardo da Vinci, the Mona Lisa is known for its mysterious smile and is one of the most visited paintings in the world.

The Scream

Created by Edvard Munch in 1893, The Scream symbolizes anxiety and strong human emotion.
