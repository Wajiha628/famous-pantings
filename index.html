Welcome

Welcome to my Famous Paintings website! This project explores some of the world's most famous works of art and the talented artists who created them. The main focus is Vincent van Gogh, whose expressive paintings continue to inspire millions.

As you explore this website, you'll learn about famous masterpieces, art history, painting techniques, and important concepts that help us appreciate art.

Featured Painting

The Starry Night was painted by Vincent van Gogh in 1889 while he lived in an asylum in Saint-Rémy, France. Today it is considered one of the greatest paintings ever created.
