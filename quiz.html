Fun Facts

  • Vincent van Gogh only sold one painting during his lifetime, yet today his work sells for millions of dollars.
  • The Mona Lisa has no visible eyebrows, which some historians believe was simply the fashion of the time.
  • The Scream exists in four different versions, created by Edvard Munch using paint, pastel, and crayon.
  • The Starry Night was painted from memory and imagination, based on the view from Van Gogh's asylum window.
